Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

This page covers the Michal's InGame Mods website (michalaferber.github.io/Modding-in-FS25) — a static landing page for a free Farming Simulator 25 mod. The short version: it collects no personal data, sets no cookies, and the mod itself runs entirely on your own machine.

What the site collects

Only privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics, via a self-hosted Plausible instance (plausible.thompsonblack.us). Plausible:

  • sets no cookies and uses no cross-site or persistent identifiers;
  • does not collect or store personal data or full IP addresses;
  • records only aggregate counts — page views, referrer, and coarse device/country — that can't be tied back to you.

There is no advertising, no fingerprinting, and no Google/Meta tracking of any kind.

Hosting

The site is served by GitHub Pages. As with any web host, GitHub may process standard request logs (including IP addresses) to deliver and protect the site; that processing is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

The mod itself

The downloadable mod runs locally inside Farming Simulator 25. It does not connect to the internet, upload anything, or phone home. Everything it does is written to your local log.txt and nowhere else.

Your choices

Because no personal data is collected and no cookies are set, there's nothing to opt out of, export, or delete. Blocking the analytics domain in your browser has no effect on the site's functionality.
